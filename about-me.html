My Journey

After building a strong technical background in IT electronics in the early 2000´s I went back to school to accquire university access rights. While studing my major Food Science and Economy I added several topics from other disciplines to my curriculum; Political Science, Ecology, Biology and Forestry Science. Being a guest student at universities in Finland, Poland and China helped me establish a link to the KNUST in Ghana to compose my Master Thesis. After finishing my studies I worked as a lecturer in Market Economics at the CAU in Kiel.
Restarting my carrer in IT in the field of project management helped my to find my current passion. Starting as a Scrum Master I realised that I want to help people find and cultivate their strengths. Therefore a formal one year long systemic business education was the next step for me.
